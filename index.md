# AI in Earth & Planetary Sciences Workshop

Welcome to the AI in Earth & Planetary Sciences (AI-EPS) workshop series. This annual workshop brings together researchers, practitioners, and students working at the intersection of artificial intelligence and Earth & Planetary Sciences.

## Current Workshop

### [2025 Workshop](2025.md)

The 2025 workshop will focus on cutting-edge applications of AI in Earth and Planetary Sciences, including:
- Machine learning for climate modeling
- Deep learning for geological image analysis
- AI-driven planetary exploration
- Predictive modeling for natural hazards
- Data-driven approaches to Earth system science

## About

The AI-EPS workshop series provides a platform for sharing research, fostering collaborations, and advancing the application of artificial intelligence techniques in Earth and Planetary Sciences. Each year, we gather experts from diverse backgrounds to discuss challenges, opportunities, and innovations in this rapidly evolving field.
